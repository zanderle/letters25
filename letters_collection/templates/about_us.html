About the project

Here is a collection of letters filled with the dreams, fears, and revelations of incredible people around the world. If you could tell your 25-year-old self anything at all, what would you say?

Mission

We believe in living life true to ourselves. We share what is important to us and inspire in each other the courage to live a life we love. Our collection of letters is not only for 20-somethings at the beginning of their careers, but also for people of all ages. Whether you are 15, 25, or 65, there is no time like now to do what you love.
